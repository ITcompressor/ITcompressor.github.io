Genearte pkcs12 store 
openssl pkcs12 -export -in certificate_test.pem -inkey privateKey_test.pem -out test.p12
Generate pkcs12 without a private key 
openssl pkcs12 -export -nokeys -in certificate.cer -out pkcs12.pfx
Extract cert from store by keytool 
keytool -exportcert -rfc -keystore server.jks -storepass password -alias server > server.pem
